Some Travel Tips for the Tipsy Traveler

I've been many places in my travels, from the far off shores of Scandanavia, to the exotic and esoteric gullies of Central Florida. I can tell you that in all of my travels, the one thing you can never have enough of is money. Money will get you out of just about any travel or fishing related bind. Always have at least $2,500 of petty cash, preferably in multiple currencies, on your person. If you do that, travel and fishing will be a breeze!
